TRACKtopia is a flexible place to record, collect, and understand personal statistics.
The goal is to support both simple events and detailed measurements, from steps,
training, sleep, and nutrition to medications, alcohol, other substances, and fully
custom tracking categories.

Beta 11 testing in progress. TRACKtopia combines general tracking, nutrition, health, activity,
training, medications, supplements, alcohol, and other substances in one local-first SQLite application.
